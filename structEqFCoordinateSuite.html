Detailed Description

The suite of functions associated with a choice of coordinates for the EqF.

Given the symmetry action of the VIO group, the matrices of the EqF are determined by the choice of local coordinates about the fixed origin. This struct provides pointers to each of those functions, and can be instantiated based on a particular choice of coordinates.
